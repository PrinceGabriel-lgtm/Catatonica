How It Works

I

Schedule your silence

Pick a duration. Block it on your calendar. Treat it with the same weight as your most important meeting — because it is. This isn't optional time. It's strategic time.

II

Enter your space

Catatonica opens. The screen clears. Choose your mode and sit with it. No prompts. No suggestions. Nothing is asking anything of you for the next N minutes.

III

Return with clarity

A gentle signal brings you back. No stats. No gamification. Just a record of the time you protected. Over weeks, the pattern becomes the practice.
